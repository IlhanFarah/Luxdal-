About Us

Luxdal is a successful small family run business which has been established in Glasgow since 2015. We are based in Queen Street. Luxdal is part of a larger and long established wax blending and refining company, so we are able to draw on their knowledge, experience and know-how from 140 years in the wax business For More Information

We are very proud that all our products are hand made using Scottish sourced companies to supply the ingredients from the wax to the packaging and proudly stamped with Made in Glasgow! We hope you find us friendly and efficient and we look forward to working with you soon.
